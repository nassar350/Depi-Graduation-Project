Our Story

Founded in 2020

Eventify was born during a time when bringing people together seemed impossible. We realized that meaningful connections and shared experiences are essential to human happiness, whether in person or virtual.

Our Mission

To democratize event creation and make it easy for anyone to bring their vision to life. We believe every gathering - from a small book club to a massive conference - deserves the tools to succeed.

Looking Forward

We're building the future of community engagement, where technology enhances human connection rather than replacing it. Join us in creating a world where amazing events happen everywhere.
